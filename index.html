Hidebar is an intelligent visibility control center for Blender. It gives you fast, convenient visibility control without digging through the Outliner. Build your own compact toggle panel for the collections, objects, and Mask modifiers you use most, and hide or reveal scene elements directly from the 3D View sidebar.

Fast Visibility Control

Keep frequently used visibility controls within reach. Toggle individual Collections, Objects, and Mask modifiers, or isolate a Collection or Object with Solo, all from one focused panel.

Video 1 Character example

Every project is different. Hide all Collections and Objects from a Panel at once. Hidebar remembers their previous state, including affected nested Collections, and restores it instead of simply turning everything back on.

Video 2 Interior example

Build Your Own Panels

Create focused Panels for a character, environment, lighting rig, product variation, or any other part of your workflow. Add only the controls you actually need and give them clear, readable names.

Video 3 Building a panel

Everything in One Place

Bring the visibility controls you use most into one compact place. Hidebar lets Collections, Objects, and Mask modifiers live side by side in the same Panel, so you can control the important parts of your scene without jumping between different areas of Blender.

Image Collections / Objects / Mask Modifiers

Export and Reuse

Export any Panel as a JSON file and import it into another .blend file. Reuse visibility setups across similar scenes, asset variations, production files, or recurring project structures.
